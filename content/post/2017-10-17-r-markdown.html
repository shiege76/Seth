library(Lahman)
library(sqldf)
library(ggplot2)
query<-"SELECT name,HR
FROM Teams
WHERE yearID=1980
ORDER BY HR"

result<-sqldf(query)

result$name<-factor(result$name,levels=result$name)

ggplot()+
  geom_bar(data=result,aes(x=name,y=HR),stat='identity',color="white",fill="blue")+
  coord_flip()+
  ylab("Homeruns")+
  xlab("Teams")+
  ggtitle("1980 Team Homerun Distribution")

The code above shows homerun totals of every team from the 1980â€™s. Usually the bar graph would not be horizontal but vertical. I used the coord_flip() command to make it horizontal.
